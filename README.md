# Northwind Traders - Executive BI Dashboard
 
An interactive Power BI dashboard built for a global gourmet food supplier, giving the executive team a clear view of global sales performance, shipping efficiency, and salesforce productivity.

## Project Overview
 
The objective was to build a BI solution using a Northwind-style relational dataset to answer five key business questions for leadership, across three dashboard pages:
 
1. **Sales & Revenue Overview** - revenue and order trends, seasonality
2. **Product & Category Intelligence** - top revenue drivers, impact of discontinued products
3. **Regional, Operational & People Performance** - market share, shipping cost/speed, employee performance


## Business Questions Answered
 
| # | Question |
|---|---|
| 1 | What are the trends in revenue and order volume over time? Are there identifiable seasonal patterns? |
| 2 | Which product categories are the top revenue drivers, and how does discontinued status impact potential sales? |
| 3 | Which countries/cities represent the highest market share, and where are freight costs disproportionately high? |
| 4 | How do shippers compare on cost and speed? Which employees lead in total sales generated? |
| 5 | How do discounts affect overall margin across product lines? |
